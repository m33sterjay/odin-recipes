Za'atar-Crusted Grilling Cheese

Description

If you feel like trying something different, this recipe is for you!

This delicious meal combines couscous, seared veggies, and delicious grilling cheese to create a very unique, but wonderful flavor.

Seriously, don't underestimate how good this is!

Ingredients

Serves 2

  • 1 - Red Onion
  • 1 - Zucchini
  • 1 - Roma Tomato
  • 1/4 oz - Fresh Cilantro
  • 1/2c - Couscous
  • 1/2 tsp - Veggie Stock Concentrate
  • 1 tsp - Sumac
  • 8 oz - Grilling Cheese
  • 1 Tbsp - Za'atar Seasoning

Steps

Enjoy!
