Chicken Gyro Couscous Bowls

Back

Description

This Mediterranean bowl is a delicious and colorful dinner that is both filling and refreshing.

The chopped salad of tomatoes, cucumber, & shallots seasoned with Dill give this a genuinely Greek flavor; while the hummus and Couscous tie everything together.

This recipe checks all the boxes!

  • Colorful? Check!
  • Crunchy? Check!
  • Creamy? Check!
  • Refreshing? Check!
  • Delicious? Check! Check!

Ingredients

Serves 2

  • 1 - Shallot
  • 1 - Roma Tomato
  • 1 - Persian Cucumber
  • 1/4oz - Fresh Dill
  • 1/2c - Couscous
  • 1/2tsp - Chicken Stock Concentrate
  • 10oz - Chicken Cutlets
  • 1 Tbsp - Za'atar Seasoning
  • 1/2c - Feta Cheese
  • 2 Tbsp - Sour Cream
  • 4 Tbsp - Hummus

Steps

  1. Prep
    • Wash and dry produce.
    • Halve and peel shallot.
    • Thinly slice one half and mince remaining.
    • Fill a small bowl with cold water, add sliced shallot and soak.
    • Dice tomato; trim and dice cucumber. Pick and finely chop fronds of Dill.

  2. Make Salad
    • Drain sliced shallot, discard liquid, and place in a medium bowl.
    • Toss with tomato, cucumber, a drizzle of olive oil, and 1 Tbsp chopped Dill.
    • Season with salt and pepper

  3. Cook Couscous
    • Heat drizzle of olive oil in a small pot over medium-high heat.
    • Add minced shallot, cook until softened; 1-2 minutes.
    • Add couscous, 1c water, stock concentrate, and 1/2 tsp salt.
    • Bring to a boil then cover and reduce to a low simmer. Cook until water has absorbed and couscous is tender; 12-15 minutes.
    • Turn off heat, keep covered until ready to serve.

  4. Cook Chicken
    • Meanwhile, pat chicken dry with paper towel, season all over with Za'atar seasoning, salt, and pepper.
    • Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
    • Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through; 3-5 minutes per side.
    • Transfer chicken to a cutting board. Once cool enough to handle, thinly slice crosswise.

  5. Make Feta Sauce
    • While chicken cooks, in a second small bowl, combine sour cream and half of the feta; mash together with a fork.
    • Add water 1 tsp at a time until mixture reaches a drizzling consistency.

  6. Serve
    • Drain any excess liquid from couscous, fluff with a fork. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Spread hummus on one side of each serving bowl; serve couscous next to hummus, top with chicken. Top hummus with salad and drizzle everything with feta sauce.
    • Garnish with remaining chopped dill if desired.

Enjoy!
