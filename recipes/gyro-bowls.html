Chicken Gyro Couscous Bowls

Description

This Mediterranean bowl is a delicious and colorful dinner that is both filling and refreshing.

The chopped salad of tomatoes, cucumber, & shallots seasoned with Dill give this a genuinely Greek flavor; while the hummus and Couscous tie everything together.
