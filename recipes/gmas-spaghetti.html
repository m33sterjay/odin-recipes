Grandma's Spaghetti

Back Gyro Bowl Grilling Cheese

Description

Believe it or not this is actually my Grandmother's spaghetti recipe!

As such, the ingredients and steps are a little less precise than the others. Feel free to use your judgement and creativity!

This has been a staple in my family for a number of generations and it is delicious and comforting.

For peak enjoyment make sure to enjoy with a side of Blueberry muffins :)

Ingredients

Serves 4

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomato Paste
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Pinch of Sugar
  • Bayleaf
  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Italian Seasoning

Steps

  1. Prepare Spaghetti
    • Begin by boiling a large pot of water.
    • When at a rolling boil, carefully submerge spaghetti into water.
    • Cover and reduce heat but keep boiling, be sure to stir occasionally to prevent sticking to the bottom of the pan.
    • Boil for 20 minutes or until al dente. Season with a pinch of salt.

  2. Cook Ground Beef
    • Heat a large drizzle of olive oil in a skillet. Chop onion and garlic.
    • Place ground beef in the skillet and begin breaking it up with a wooden spoon until satisfied; season with salt and pepper.
    • When beef is 75% browned, throw in onion and garlic. Cook until onion is translucent and beef is browned.
    • When beef is browned, remove skillet from heat and drain excess fat.
    • Return beef to the skillet.

  3. Make the Sauce
    • Pour spaghetti sauce and tomato sauce into skillet with the ground beef and onions.
    • Mix in tomato paste until completely mixed.
    • Add a pinch of sugar and salt. Mix in garlic powder and italian seasoning to taste.
    • Carefully submerge 4 bayleaf leaves in the sauce. Leave mixed in until ready to serve, then remove from sauce and discard.
    • Simmer sauce on low heat until reduced and a consistency you prefer. Be sure to stir occasionally to avoid burning.

  4. Serve
    • Combine sauce and spaghetti in serving bowl and serve generous portions.
    • For best results, serve with blueberry muffins and salad!

Enjoy!
